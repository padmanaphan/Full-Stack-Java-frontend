Loading Bays Management

The Loading Bays module allows you to monitor and manage the physical infrastructure of the warehouse docks. You can track which bays are available, occupied, or under maintenance. Keep real-time tabs on the current weight at each bay to prevent overloading and ensure warehouse safety.

View All Bays

Get a complete overview of all loading bays, their types, current statuses, and weight limits.

Create Bay

Register a new inbound, outbound, or cold storage bay into the warehouse layout.

Update Weight

Record the current weight occupying a loading bay during a loading/unloading operation.

Update Status

Change the status of a bay to maintenance, available, reserved, or occupied.
