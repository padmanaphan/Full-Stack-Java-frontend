Shipment Management

The Shipments module is the core of DockDash's logistics tracking. It allows Logistics Coordinators and Dock Managers to register new shipments arriving at or departing from the warehouse. You can monitor priority, track hazardous materials, and manage the flow of goods to optimize warehouse efficiency.

View Shipments

Browse the shipment registry, filter by availability, and remove completed shipments.

Create Shipment

Register a new inbound or outbound shipment to the warehouse system.
